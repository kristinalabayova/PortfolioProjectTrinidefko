Trinidefko

Electronic Music Club in CA

About

Trinidefko is a large venue holding up to 1000 people which puts on cracking club nights with the freshest DJs and performers out there. Amongst the high profile house, techno, trance, drum and bass and excellent dubstep nights where you can rave until the early morning at this south California party club. Voted the 3nd best club in the world by DJ Santua after NightClubTridet. With two large rooms and the music style combinations variety we have been pleased to be so popular. You can expect any night at Trinidefko to be one of the best nights out you’ve ever had.

Trini

Room 1’s ‘dnbbeats’ dancefloor is one of our’s unique features as 450 bass transducers are attached to the floor to give visitors a true full body experience.

Defko

Room 2 provides an underground feel with its near pitch black atmosphere and hidden enclaves where you rave to the music away, from the crowds.
